Our Story

Who we are.

HomeGram Estate Pvt Ltd is a premium plotted-developments company headquartered in Gurugram. Founded in 2014, we are not a brokerage, not a marketplace, and not a volume developer. We are a land advisory — selecting, developing, and delivering plots that we'd buy ourselves.

Over a decade, we have served 500+ families — doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, NRIs — all looking for the same thing: land with clear title, genuine infrastructure, and long-term appreciation backed by real demand drivers.

We evaluate seven parcels for every one we select. What you see — Bahadurgarh and Lansdowne — is what survived our diligence. Everything else was rejected.

Vision & Philosophy

Three pillars.
One standard.

Every decision at HomeGram is filtered through three non-negotiable principles. They are why 500+ families trust us — and why we've turned away far more business than we've accepted.

01 / Principle

Curation over Volume

We don't list 50 projects. We develop two. Both selected after months of market analysis, title diligence, infrastructure mapping, and growth thesis validation. Less is more when the stakes are high.

02 / Principle

Infrastructure before Promises

Roads, electricity, drainage, boundary walls, parks — all built before plot handover. We don't promise future infrastructure. We deliver current infrastructure. That's the HomeGram difference.

03 / Principle

Advisory, not Transaction

We don't "sell" plots. We advise on land investment. The relationship doesn't end at registry — we're available for resale guidance, construction referrals, and portfolio reviews for life.
