Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Is the land freehold? Can I get a bank loan?+
Yes. All plots are freehold ownership with clean titles. Registry happens at government sub-registrar rates. Bank loans are available — we assist with documentation for all major banks including SBI, HDFC, and ICICI.
What's included with the plot?+
Every plot comes within a gated society with internal roads (20–40 ft), underground drainage, electricity connections, boundary walls, landscaped parks, community center, and 24/7 security with CCTV. Not raw land — a complete residential environment.
What are the payment options?+
Flexible plans: (1) Full payment with discount, (2) Construction-linked plan (pay in stages as development progresses), (3) EMI via bank loan starting from ₹15,000/month. No penalty for early closure.
When do I need to build a house?+
There's no construction deadline. Buy the plot now at pre-launch prices, and build whenever suits your timeline — 1 year, 5 years, or 10 years from now. Your land appreciates regardless of whether you build.
Is this RERA registered?+
Yes. Our projects are RERA-compliant (registration in progress for pre-launch phase). RERA number will be shared at booking. All documentation is transparent and legally verified before any handover.
Can I visit the site before booking?+
Absolutely. We arrange free site visits every weekend for Bahadurgarh (pickup from Delhi Metro) and monthly group trips to Lansdowne. Register your interest and we'll schedule a visit within 7 days.
